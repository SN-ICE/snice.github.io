Retreat2024

The 2024 SN group retreat

We will meet in Vallgorguina (Vallès Oriental) for three days (April 9-11) to finish up a couple of on-going but in an advance stage projects. We will also have a few short hands-on sessions on tools group members have either developed or are using for their research, and we will have time for a couple of Walking-Working-Excursions (WWE).

More details can be found in the Retreat2024 github repo. 

Participants (alph.): Alaa Alburai, Noor Ali, Dane Cross, Lluís Galbany, Claudia Gutiérrez, Maider González, Cristina Jiménez, Maria Kopsacheili, Tomás Müller-Bravo, Kim Phan.
