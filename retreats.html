Retreats

The 2025 SN group retreat

For the second time, we will meet in Vallgorguina (Vallès Oriental) for three days (March 11-13) to learn HOSTPHOT and Prospector, the two codes to measure photometry and perform stellar population synthesis that our group uses the most. This knowledge will be applied to several SN-galaxy surveys we need to analyse for our individual projects: PISCO, AMUSING, FLOWS, and SAINTS. Github repo.

The 2024 SN group retreat

The first SN-ICE group retreat. We met in Vallgorguina (Vallès Oriental) for three days (April 9-11) to pursue two projects: the analysis of early SN spectra with GTC, and the NIR SNIa siblings study. In addition we had a few short hands-on sessions on tools group members have either developed or are using for their research. Github repo.
