Retreat2025

The 2025 SN group retreat

We will meet in Vallgorguina (Vallès Oriental) for three days (March 11-13) to learn HOSTPHOT and Prospector, the two codes to measure photometry and perform stellar population synthesis that our group uses the most. This knowledge will be applied to several SN-galaxy surveys we need to analyse for our individual projects: PISCO, AMUSING, FLOWS, and SAINTS. We will also have time for a couple of Walking-Working-Excursions (WWE).

More details can be found in the Retreat2025 github repo. 

Participants (alph.): Alaa Alburai, Dane Cross, Lluís Galbany, Claudia Gutiérrez, Maider González, Cristina Jiménez, Maria Kopsacheili, Hao-Yu Miao, Kim Phan, Martin Solar.
